Vehicle Fleet Maintenance Application Redesign

Project Overview

I was asked to help "make all products easier to use" and develop a strategy to provide a consistent UI/UX as well.The first product I started redesigning first was the vehicle fleet maintenance application which in its current state only offered a non responsive desktop application.

The samples displayed here show the near final creative for the overall design. Part way through this project, I was asked to also develop a companion mobile app for this product (mobile app design samples also displayed here).

Challenges & Solutions

There were several challenges to overcome including: no documented user research or persona profiles, and internally the organization had no experience with UX research methods or processes.

Solving challenges included: working with product manager, customer support to understand the most common and/or critical tasks users require support for; recommending and documenting user research methods and processes; developing UX research tests and participants; working directly with users throughout UX research testing and concept validation; internally socialization and education regarding UI/UX design in general, user centered design; UX research, and other areas.

Requirements

  • Develop an overall application redesign direction
  • Design a new header, footer and content styling that will offer a global UI/UX consistency througought all SaaS platform applications
  • Coordinate with our marketing team to ensure consistency between product design and corporate communications
  • Design a prototype for the companion mobile app (view prototype)

Technology

  • Agile Scrum
  • HTML, CSS, jQuery, Bootstrap, SASS
  • MS PowerPoint, MS Excel, Google Forms
