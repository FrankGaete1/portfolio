Power Utility

Role

Sr. UX Designer and Researcher @ NextEra/Florida Power & Light, 2020-2021

NextEra is an industry-leading company, focused on innovation, customer value and energy solutions.

NextEra Energy, Inc. (NEE) owns Florida Power & Light (FPL), which has two nuclear power plants in Florida that generate more than 3,000 megawatts of clean, emission-free energy. This is enough power to supply nearly 1.9 million homes. FPL's nuclear operations also create thousands of jobs and more than $1.4 billion in economic benefits for Florida.

Responsibilities

  • Designed interfaces, experiences and content for nuclear power enterprise applications
  • Planned and executed UX research; analyzed data to guide design enhancement recommendations; helped standardize UX research methods and processes for future testing
  • Developed new design patterns and workflows using Adobe XD, Sketch, and UXpin
  • Collaborated with nuclear division team members; product owners and enterprise UX designers
