Case Study: TD Bank's Payment Center

TD Bank

TD Bank, N.A. is an American national bank and the United States subsidiary of the Canadian multinational TD Bank Group.

TD Bank has received numerous awards and recognitions for its various initiatives and performance, including being named Best Consumer Digital Bank in North America for the fourth consecutive year by Global Finance, and winning the 2025 BIG Innovation Awards for the fourth year in a row

Project Overview

PROBLEM: At the time I joined TD Bank, the only way a TD credit card holder could make payments toward their credit card was by transfering money from a deposit account to their credit card account. There was no way for the user to choose to pay statement/current balance or a minimum amount due, no way to set up automatic payments, schedule future one time payments or perform many related functions that competitors already had in place.

SOLUTION/REQUIREMENT: Design and build a service/feature to allow TD credit card account holders to make payments to their card(s) with all standard payment options. I recommended creating a payment center that would not only allow credit card payments but in the future be leveraged by other TD payment accounts such as auto and mortgage loans.

Discovery

  • Understand user needs, motivations, behaviors, and pain points
  • Methods:
    • Review existing credit card payment workflows, user journeys, stakeholder insights, competitive analysis
    • Research: analyze existing customer feedback, create initial wireframe concepts of new payment workflows

Definition & Analysis

  • Distill research findings into actionable insights.
  • Methods:
    • Review or create user personas
    • Empathy maps, user journey maps
    • Create defined problem statements

Ideation

  • Develop potential solutions for defined problems.
  • Methods:
    • Sketch, create wireframes
    • Map user flows
    • Develop information architecture
    • Collobaration sessions with other team members

Prototyping

  • Convert ideas into interactive (clickable) prototypes that can be used for internal concept validation.
  • Methods:
    • Convert approved wireframe concepts into high definition designs and clickable prototypes
    • Collaborate with UX Research team to define what we want to learn and what to test
    • Perform usability studies to test and validate new concepts

Usability Testing & Validation

  • Validate designs with real users to identify usability issues.
  • Convert ideas into interactive (clickable) prototypes that can be used for testable and concept validation.
  • Use resulting prototyping test data to identify gaps/issues early on and adust accordingly.

Implementation - Developer Handoff

  • Hand off design assets to developers assigned to build the product/feature to be released.
  • Methods:
    • Prepare design specs and assets (Figma Dev mode)
    • Provide documentation to support and guide developers

Post-Launch Evaluation

  • Monitor performance after product/feature goes live.
  • Methods:
    • Analyze post launch user feedback and metrics such as adoption rate
    • Perform new usability study to validate release and inform design enhance recommendations for the next release
